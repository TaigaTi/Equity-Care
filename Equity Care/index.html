No Matter Who You Are Or Where You Come From, We'll Take Care Of You

Health Disparities are a growing concern in today's society. These disparities are usually caused by one of several factors some of which include race, ethnicity, social status, geographical location and financial standing. Here at Equity Care, we strive to alleviate the health disparities which may be linked to these factors through our affordable Online Health Center. We hope to ease these roadblocks to healthcare through our easily accessible online consultations with our culturally sensitive doctors as well as through education with our Tip Centre.

Who

Many persons around the world lack access to healthcare because of where they may live. Their address prohibits basic health checkups and doctor visits and means that these necessities become far-fetched for these individuals. These disadvantages mean that persons tend to have several health issues that remain undiagnosed and therefore untreated. Equity Care hopes to alleviate the number of persons who may be faced with this unfortunate reality.

What

A health disparity can be defined as a difference in health between persons that can be attributed to one or more of the following factors:

  • Social Disadvantages such as race, ethnicity and gender
  • Economic Disadvantages such as lack of income and expensive health care
  • Environmental Disadvantages such as lack of easy access to a medical center or health care facility

Where

All over the world, health disparities tend to occur in rural and urban areas. Any health care services that may be provided nearby are usually far too expensive for these persons of lower income. As a result, many persons will choose to neglect their own health by putting off doctor visits and instead put that money into putting food on the table.

When

Health disparities have been a prevalent issues in our society for as long as history can remember. It is well known that in the days of slavery and shortly thereafter, persons of color were neglected and their health was not of major concern. As a result, the mortality rate for persons of colour were unbelievably high. Over approxiamately 17 years, the death rate for African Americans has declined by about 25% , mainly for those over the age of 65. However, disparities in health still remain a growing concern in today's environment. In the year 2020, the Corona Virus Pandemic struck the world, raising concerns about health and well-being through the roof. This virus is known to target especially those with underlying health conditions and because many persons neglecting their health may suffer from these conditions, this calls for immediate action.

Why

Unfortunately, distance is one of the greatest barriers in terms of access to healthcare and as such, constributes significantly to health disparities among persons who may live in areas without easy access to this necessity. This may result in many persons leaving their respective illnesses undiagnosed and untreated as they simply have no where to go to seek help. We at Equity Care are hoping to alleviate this issue through a 100% online health center.

Business Background

Equity Care is an Online Health Consultancy Service which aims to equalize health disparities due to race, ethnicity and geographical location. Equity Care removes the barrier of these disadvantages by catering to those who typically experience greater obstacles in obtaining health care. Through our online doctors appointments with culturally sensitive doctors, online training sessions with cultural music and dances and our Tip Centre which provides educational tips on common health issues, we hope to better the health of persons all over the world.

Our Services

At Equity Care, we offer online health care services in the form of Online Doctor Consultation, Online Workout Sessions and a Tip Centre. Our Online Consultantations are conducted by certified medical practitioners, some of which are fluent in several languages and thereby able to cater to the needs of persons from all over the world.

Our Online Workout Sessions are accompanied with a wide variety of cultural music to help persons feel included and excited about getting in shape. Some of these training sessions may even include dance sessions which incorporate different dance moves and routines from all over. The Tip Centre Aims to educate persons on common health issues and ways to prevent them. This is in an effort to combat the fact that a lack of education is a common factor in many person's poor health choices.
